Run Monster Run

Run Monster Run is a survival/top-down/19th-century-set video game, where you play a newly created Frankenstein's monster just escaping from his confinements of his masters castle laboratory and is now on the run from unhappy townsfolk. Fight your way through angry villagers, obstructive terrain and painful bandsaws.
